Our Story

From the mountains to the coast.

Brooks was born from a simple idea: South Africa deserves a hard seltzer as wild and beautiful as its landscape. We started at the foot of the Hottentots-Holland mountains, where cold mountain water meets fynbos air, and asked — what if we bottled that feeling?

Every can of Brooks starts with pristine South African spring water, naturally carbonated and paired with botanicals sourced from our coastal valleys and subtropical forests. No compromises. No shortcuts.

We make Brooks for the sundowner braais, the early morning surf checks, the summit celebrations and the lazy afternoon floats. Wherever the moment finds you — Brooks is there.
